About

Boral Hall

This is a six-storeyed hall for the accommodation of male students of BAUET. The hall is named after the prominent river ‘Boral’ of Natore. It has a seat capacity of 466. The hall provides various facilities such as modern tiled rooms, steel cots, mattresses, shoe racks, modern toilets, geysers, Wi-Fi, newspapers, a hygienic kitchen and dining room, purified water, a TV, indoor and outdoor recreational facilities, a standard cook with hygienic food at competitive prices, CCTV, PA system, biometric access control, 24/7 security, a prayer room, reading room, standby generator and medical facilities.

The hall is located within the campus of BAUET, surrounded by a beautiful garden and a pond. It features a large playground for outdoor games and sports, a library with a good collection of books and journals, a computer lab with internet access, a gymnasium and a common room for indoor games and other recreational activities.

Boral Hall is committed to providing a safe, secure, and comfortable living environment. A strict code of conduct is enforced, and a resident advisor is appointed to maintain discipline and order.

Boral Hall is more than a residence—it's a place where students live, learn, and grow together. It offers opportunities to build friendships, develop leadership skills and participate in extracurricular activities, promoting responsibility, respect and support among peers.
